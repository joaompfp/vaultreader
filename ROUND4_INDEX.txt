KEY INSIGHTS: WHAT ROUND 4 LEARNED
================================================================================

1. MONOLITHIC BEATS SEGMENTED
   ├─ Single unified form > multiple parts
   ├─ Political symbols are BOLD and UNIFIED
   └─ No internal detail = stronger icon

2. FORM MATTERS MORE THAN COORDINATES
   ├─ Fractional coords don't create "organic feel"
   ├─ THE SHAPE ITSELF must be naturally curved
   ├─ Asymmetric bezier handles = hand-drawn aesthetic
   └─ Perfect geometry = computer-generated feel

3. 28px-FIRST DESIGN
   ├─ Design for actual render size, not theoretical
   ├─ If detail disappears at 28px, remove it
   ├─ Bold silhouette > decorative complexity
   └─ Specification is law

4. POLITICAL AUTHENTICITY
   ├─ Fist = solidarity, labor, personal power (real symbol)
   ├─ Hammer = labor movement, craft (authentic icon)
   ├─ Star = revolution, communism (ideological symbol)
   └─ Corporate UI icons fail the "banner test"

5. SIMPLICITY IS STRENGTH
   ├─ Every pixel must serve a purpose
   ├─ At 28px, less is more
   ├─ Monolithic > ornamental
   └─ Political > decorative
