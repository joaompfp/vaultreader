## Design Strategy

### PESSOAL (Personal Life) — Redesign to FIST

**Rationale:**
- House is too corporate (Airbnb, Google Home aesthetic)
- **FIST** = universal symbol of personal agency, resistance, solidarity
- **Better political DNA** — recognizable as personal power/reclamation
- **Monolithic** — single bold silhouette, impossible to segment
- **Hand-drawn** — organic curves on knuckles, wrist naturally create irregularity

**28px Strategy:**
- Simple closed-fist shape
- Thumb slightly forward/down (organic positioning)
- Knuckle bumps with subtle curves (hand-drawn feel)
- Wrist tapering naturally to base

### WORK (Professional/Labor) — Redesign to FIST or HAMMER RADICAL RETHINK

**Critical Issue:** Current hammer unreadable at 28px. Two options:

**OPTION A: Switch to FIST (same as PESSOAL but different positioning)**
- Different hand orientation (palm forward vs knuckles out)
- This creates visual distinction while keeping monolithic form

**OPTION B: Radically redesign HAMMER**
- Make it a MONOLITHIC single silhouette
- Hammer head + handle = ONE unified form (not two parts)
- Design for 28px: massive blocky head, thin handle just visible
- Add hand-drawn curves to both head and handle junction

**Choice:** Going with **FIST (palm forward, fingers extended upward)** for distinctiveness
- **PESSOAL**: Closed fist (personal agency)
- **WORK**: Open fist (solidarity, labor power, resistance)

### PROJECTS (Innovation/Energy) — Redesign STAR with hand-drawn feel

**Keep:** 5-pointed star (connects to PCP symbolism)
**Remove:** Spark (invisible at 28px)
**Add:** Hand-drawn organic feel to star itself
- Imperfect angles (not 72° perfectly)
- Slightly wobbly edges (hand-traced aesthetic)
- Subtle curve variations on points
- Test: Looks traced from hand sketch, not generated by math

**Alternative if star too plain:** Add subtle inner cutout (like PCP star) using `fill-rule="evenodd"`
