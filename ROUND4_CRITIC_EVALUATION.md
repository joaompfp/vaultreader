## Detailed Icon Critique

### Icon 1: PESSOAL (Leaf) — ❌ REJECTED

**Score: 2/5 (Fail)**

#### Readability at 28px
- **Result:** ❌ **POOR**
- The 28px render shows two stacked ovals that read as "8" or a generic abstract symbol, NOT a leaf
- Zero leaf-like cues (no stem, no pointed tip, no blade structure)
- The gap between the ovals breaks the illusion entirely at small scale
- **Verdict:** Fails the basic readability requirement

#### Visual Boldness
- **Result:** ⚠️ **WEAK**
- The fragmented two-oval structure lacks visual weight
- Feels like a subtle app icon, not a protest banner symbol
- Does NOT pass the visual gravity test ("Would this appear on a strike poster?")

#### Monolithic Form
- **Result:** ❌ **CRITICAL FAILURE**
- The icon is **explicitly split into TWO DISCONNECTED SHAPES**
- This violates the core design principle: "monolithic bold forms"
- A true monolithic leaf would be one unified silhouette, not two separated pieces

#### Organic Coordinates & Hand-Drawn Feel
- **Result:** ⚠️ **TOO GEOMETRIC**
- The two ovals are perfectly symmetrical and smooth
- Feels like computer-generated vector shapes, not hand-traced
- Zero irregularity = zero organic feeling

#### Political Aesthetic
- **Result:** ❌ **GENERIC**
- This does NOT evoke SOS Racismo's organic hand gesture
- Does NOT feel like a political movement graphic
- Could be from a wellness app or nature brand

#### Specific Feedback
1. **The core problem:** Two ovals instead of one unified leaf shape
2. **The 28px problem:** Loses all resemblance to a leaf at actual render size
3. **The aesthetic problem:** Feels sterile and corporate, not political

### Icon 3: PROJECTS (Lightning) — ⚠️ NEEDS REFINEMENT

**Score: 4/5 (Good, but needs organic improvement)**

#### Readability at 28px
- **Result:** ✅ **MODERATE-TO-GOOD**
- At 28px, it reads as a stylized lightning bolt OR a generic star
- The silhouette is visible, but the interpretation isn't 100% certain
- Sharp angles help, but may blur at very small scales
- **Acceptable, but could be clearer**

#### Visual Boldness
- **Result:** ✅ **STRONG**
- Sharp angular form + red color = high visual impact
- Association with urgency and action (good for "projects")
- Would work on a protest poster (though it might read more as "energy" than specific action)

#### Monolithic Form
- **Result:** ✅ **SUCCEEDS**
- Despite angular complexity, it's a single continuous filled shape
- Sharp points integrate into unified silhouette (not fragmented)
- No internal breaks or decorative elements

#### Organic Coordinates & Hand-Drawn Feel
- **Result:** ⚠️ **TOO GEOMETRIC** (Critical Issue)
- Fractional coordinates exist (17.2, 1.3; 17.6, 0.8; 18.4, 0.9, etc.)
- **BUT:** The angles are perfectly symmetrical and sharp
- Looks like digitally-generated geometry, not hand-drawn
- Zero irregularity in the pointed tips
- Lacks the subtle curve variations that make hand-traced graphics feel authentic

#### Political Aesthetic
- **Result:** ✅ **GOOD**
- Lightning = energy, action, revolutionary power
- Bold form fits political graphics
- But falls slightly short of SOS Racismo's **organic, natural** hand-drawn aesthetic

#### Specific Feedback for Refinement
1. **The geometry problem:** Replace razor-sharp points with slightly rounded tips or organic micro-curves
2. **The asymmetry problem:** Add subtle irregularities to the angles (not perfect 45° corners)
3. **The hand-drawn test:** Does it look like it was carefully hand-traced? Currently: NO. Should be: YES.

#### Example refinement
Instead of perfectly symmetric points, introduce subtle variations:
- Upper point at (17.2, 1.3) could shift slightly (17.3, 1.2) to suggest natural drawing
- Side points should have micro-curves rather than razor angles
- Bottom point should feel weighted, not knife-sharp

#### Strengths
- Monolithic form ✓
- Strong visual presence ✓
- 28px readable ✓
- Good political association ✓

#### Weaknesses
- Too geometric, not organic ✗
- Lacks hand-drawn aesthetic ✗
- Symmetrical perfection (should be imperfect) ✗

#### Recommendation
**⚠️ MINOR REFINEMENT** — Soften angles, introduce organic micro-curves, add subtle asymmetry to pass the "hand-traced" visual DNA test.
