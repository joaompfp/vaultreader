VISUAL DNA COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

✅ FILLED SILHOUETTES
   All icons: zero stroke attributes, 100% filled paths
   
✅ ORGANIC COORDINATES
   pessoal: 16.3, 19.3, 28.4, 26.0, 13.8, 29.0
   work: 16.8, 18.2, 13.4, 19.2, 18.7, 21.8, 15.9, 25.2
   projects: 16.2, 20.8, 18.1, 30.3, 31.6, 4.8, 5.2, 6.2
   → Never perfect integers, simulating hand-traced geometry

✅ ASYMMETRIC BEZIER HANDLES
   All curves: handle distances vary (e.g., 2.5px vs 3.8px)
   → Creates "hand-drawn by stamp" organic feel

✅ WARM CMYK RED (#b91c1c)
   Matches SOS Racismo (#9c1c1f) and PCP (#ed1c24) aesthetic
   NOT pure #ff0000 (which is harsh, computer-like)

✅ MONOLITHIC BOLD FORMS
   pessoal: unified house silhouette
   work: merged hammer head + handle
   projects: main star + accent spark
   → Each is single, unified aesthetic, not collage of pieces

✅ READABLE AT 28x28px

   All icons instantly recognizable at tiny size
