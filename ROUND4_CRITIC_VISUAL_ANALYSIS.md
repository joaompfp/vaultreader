- **Does it read as LEAF?** NO
- **Does it feel iconic?** NO

#### Design Issues Identified

**Issue 1: Two Disconnected Shapes**
- The icon is split into TWO SEPARATE OVALS (not one unified form)
- This violates the **monolithic form principle** — core to SOS Racismo + PCP visual DNA
- Two shapes feel like a collage, not a symbol

**Issue 2: Over-Symmetry**
- Both ovals are perfectly smooth and symmetrical
- Curves are perfectly circular (not organic)
- Feels like computer-generated geometry, not hand-traced
- Zero irregularity = zero authenticity

**Issue 3: Lost at Small Scale**
- At 28px, the gap between ovals becomes invisible
- The silhouette collapses into an ambiguous blob
- Fails the basic usability requirement (must be recognizable at actual render size)

**Issue 4: Missing Leaf Identity**
- A leaf needs a TIP (pointed, recognizable)
- A leaf needs a STEM (base structure)
- A leaf needs a BLADE (curved sides showing growth)
- This design has NONE of these

#### Political Aesthetic Test

- ❌ Would this appear on a nature/environmental poster? MAYBE (but not as iconic)

**FAILS ALL TESTS** — Must be completely redesigned as a single monolithic leaf shape.

#### At 160px (Large Scale)
- **Appearance:** Bold zigzag lightning bolt
- **What it reads as:** Lightning bolt OR a generic star (slightly ambiguous)
- **Iconic presence:** Good, energetic, but feels digital

#### At 28px (Actual Render Size)
- **Readability:** MODERATE
- **First impression:** "Lightning bolt? Or a star? It's... energetic anyway"
- **Does it read as LIGHTNING?** MAYBE (recognizable, but could be clearer)
- **Does it feel iconic?** YES (good visual presence)

#### Design Issues Identified

**Issue 1: Razor-Sharp Angles**
- All corners are perfectly sharp (90°, 45°, etc.)
- The points feel knife-like, not hand-drawn
- Perfectly symmetric geometry = feels digitally-generated
- Missing the organic micro-curves that make SOS/PCP logos feel authentic

**Issue 2: Perfect Geometry**
- Every angle is mathematically perfect
- No subtle irregularities or hand-drawn variations
- Feels like it came from Adobe Illustrator's "auto-smooth", not a human hand

**Issue 3: Lack of Asymmetry**
- Left side is perfectly mirrored to right side
- A hand-drawn bolt would have slight variations
- The perfection kills the "political poster" feeling

#### Design Strengths

**Strength 1: Monolithic Form**
- Despite complexity, it's ONE continuous shape
- No breaks, no separate elements
- Unified silhouette feels intentional

**Strength 2: 28px Readability**
- Despite sharpness, the bolt shape is recognizable
- The silhouette is clear and distinct
- Doesn't become illegible at small scale

**Strength 3: Strong Visual Presence**
- Sharp angles = high visual energy
- Bold red silhouette = high impact
- Good association with action, urgency, projects

**Strength 4: Good Political Association**
- Lightning = energy, action, revolutionary power, bright ideas
- Fits the "projects" concept well
- Works as a protest symbol (just needs organic refinement)

#### Political Aesthetic Test
- ⚠️ Would this appear on a protest banner? ALMOST (good energy, but needs organic feel)
- ⚠️ Would this appear on a revolutionary poster? ALMOST (needs hand-drawn polish)
- ✅ Does it feel like a political movement graphic? YES (with refinement potential)

#### Refinement Path

The PROJECTS icon is 80% correct. The concept is solid. It just needs:

1. **Soften sharp angles** → Replace razor corners with micro-curves (0.5–1px radius)
2. **Add asymmetry** → Make left and right sides slightly different
3. **Add organic micro-variations** → Introduce subtle imperfections
4. **Result:** Same bolt shape, but feels "hand-traced" not "computer-generated"

This is a 30-minute refinement, not a redesign.

#### Verdict
**GOOD WITH MINOR REFINEMENT NEEDED** — Concept is strong, execution needs organic polish.
