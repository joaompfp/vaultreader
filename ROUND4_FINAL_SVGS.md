## Designer Notes

These 3 icons represent a **paradigm shift** from Round 2-3 approach:

- **NOT** corporate UI icons (Material Design, Font Awesome style)
- **ARE** political movement graphics (SOS Racismo, PCP style)
- **NOT** decorated or ornamental (no spark, no veins)
- **ARE** bold, monolithic, authentic symbols
- **NOT** compromised for detail at large scales
- **ARE** optimized for 28px readability (actual use case)

The fist, hammer, and star carry **ideological weight**. They would appear on protest banners, not in a tech startup's design system.
