## Critical Insight: Visual Gravity Problem

The breakthrough finding: Icons fail not because they're unreadable, but because they **lack political gravity**.

**The Visual Gravity Test:**
Would this design appear on a protest banner or revolutionary flag?

- ✓ SOS hand — YES (solidarity gesture)
- ✓ PCP star — YES (revolutionary power)
- ✓ Hammer — YES (labor, solidarity)
- ✗ House — NO (generic utility shape)
- ✗ Star+Sparks — NO (decorative, ornamental)

**Diagnosis:**
- **House:** Too generic, appears in every design system, no political meaning
- **Star+Sparks:** Suffers from "decorative ornament disease" — adding tiny spark details instead of bold monolithic form
- **Hammer:** Correct — bold, meaningful, fills space, carries weight
