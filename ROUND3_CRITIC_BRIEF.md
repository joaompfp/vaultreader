## Red Flags (Deal-Breakers)

If you see ANY of these, the icon needs revision:

- ❌ **Perfect integers throughout** (e.g., M16 L32 Z) — kills organic feel
- ❌ **Thick strokes instead of fills** — violates visual DNA
- ❌ **Illegible at 28px** — fails basic requirement
- ❌ **Pure #ff0000 instead of warm red** — wrong political aesthetic
- ❌ **Generic corporate look** — feels like a SaaS app icon, not political graphics
- ❌ **More than 3 paths per icon** — violates simplicity constraint
- ❌ **Ambiguous silhouette** — doesn't clearly read as house/hammer/star
