## Design Decisions: Reasoning

### Why Leaf Over House?
**Problem:** House is ubiquitous in app design. Every software has a "house" home icon. Zero political meaning.

**Solution:** Leaf represents GROWTH, personal development, and LIFE.
- ✅ Meaningful symbolism (environmental + personal)
- ✅ Organic curved form (matches SOS Racismo aesthetic)
- ✅ Would work on a solidarity or environmental poster
- ✅ Fills 70% of viewBox (visual gravity)

**Alternative Considered:** Monumental house (much bolder), but leaf is more meaningful.

### Why Hammer+ (Not Redesign)?
**Reasoning:** Hammer was already excellent (8/10). No need to change concept, just enhance.

**Enhancement Strategy:**
- +8% head width (6.8–18.2 vs 7.2–17.8)
- +11% head height (8.1 vs 7.3)
- Wider handle presence (17.2 vs 17.0 start)
- Result: 9/10 visual gravity

**Why Not Redesign?** Hammer is a revolutionary labor symbol. No other icon conveys "work" as powerfully. Minor refinement = optimal outcome.

### Why Lightning Over Star+Sparks?
**Problem:** Star+sparks violate monolithic form principle. Sparks are decorative ornament, not bold forms.

**Solution:** Lightning bolt = SPEED, ENERGY, ACTION, INNOVATION.
- ✅ Single unified monolithic path (zero separate shapes)
- ✅ Meaningful symbolism (revolutionary energy, action)
- ✅ Bold zigzag fills 75% of viewBox (visual gravity)
- ✅ Would work on a revolutionary/action poster

**Why Lightning Over Chunky Star (Alternative)?** Lightning is more energetic, more distinct from PCP star, better suits "projects/innovation" theme.

## Lessons Learned

### What Worked
1. **Meaningful Symbolism Over Generic Utility** — Leaf > House because it has meaning
2. **Monolithic Forms Are Powerful** — Lightning > Star+Sparks because unified form = better visual impact
3. **Minor Refinements on Good Designs** — Hammer+ shows that sometimes small changes (boldness) = big impact
4. **Visual Gravity Test Is Key** — "Would this work on a banner?" is the ultimate arbiter of good design

### Critical Insights
- Political movement graphics MUST have meaning (solidarity, labor, energy, growth)
- Decorative ornaments (sparkles, details) kill visual gravity
- Fractional coordinates + asymmetric bezier = organic hand-traced aesthetic
- Fills 65–75% of viewBox = visual dominance without excess
